Design Concept D

Key Highlights

  • Components (man power detect, signal transmit, control, drive motor, and drive force transmit) are arranged around Axle of the hub and stationary plate of each drive wheel.
  • Electric driving unit is located at the front portion of the bottom support posts.
  • Electric driving unit includes outer box, battery, two motors, and control box.
  • Wheelchair employs Four-wheel configuration (two front electric drive wheels, two rear wheels).
Requirement Features & Specifications
Compact and Integrated Design
  • Components (man power detect, signal transmit, control, drive motor, and drive force transmit) are arranged around Axle of the hub and stationary plate of each drive wheel.
  • Electric driving unit is located at the front portion of the bottom support posts.
  • Electric driving unit includes outer box, battery, two motors, and control box.
Enhanced Mobility and Navigation
  • Wheelchair employs Four-wheel configuration (two front electric drive wheels, two rear wheels).
  • Electric Drive Wheels are located at Front portion of the bottom support posts.
  • Control System enables Autonomous following capability based on sensor data and mobile identification device.
  • Control System uses Closed-loop feedback system using armature voltage and current to measure actual motor speed.
  • Obstacle Avoidance System includes Obstacle avoidance sensor.
  • Wheelchair can navigate Various surfaces like beds, toilets, and hospital beds.
  • Wheelchair has Adjustable height and reach.
Reliable Power Supply
  • Battery provides Power supply.
  • Battery powers Electric motors and control systems.
Simplified Maintenance and Repair
  • Controller allows for Easy removal or replacement.
  • Clutch switches Power transmission path from the electric motor to the drivable wheel between an engagement state and a disengagement state.
  • Controller is Integrated control system.
  • Wiring is Compact and reduced in length.
  • Design is Integrated around the wheel hub for simplified manufacture and assembly.
Stable and Safe Operation
  • Elbow and Bottom Support Posts are arranged in Parallel.
  • Bottom Plate connects Car body support posts.
  • Frame is configured to Support a seat.
  • Controller is located on At least one armrest for controlling the entire electric wheelchair.
  • Comparing Circuit subtractively combines Armature voltage and reference signal to produce a first difference signal.
User Comfort and Support
  • Seat is Foldable.
  • Seat is located in Middle of the body bracket.
  • Armrests are located on Each side of the seat.
  • Armrests provide Structural support for the user during transfer.
  • Standing Platform is connected to Bottom of the body bracket.
  • Suspension System absorbs Shocks and vibrations from uneven terrain.
