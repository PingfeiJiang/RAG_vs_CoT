Design Concept C

Key Highlights

  • Drive Train Assembly is drive train assembly 110.
  • Control System executes obstacle avoidance maneuvers.
  • Brake Pads make mechanical braking contact with wheels to provide steering.
  • Computational Module receives electrical signals for navigation.
Requirement Features & Specifications
Autonomous Navigation and Obstacle Avoidance
  • Control System executes obstacle avoidance maneuvers.
  • Brake Pads make mechanical braking contact with wheels to provide steering.
  • Computational Module receives electrical signals for navigation.
  • Computational Module determines mechanical braking contact to generate slowing and turning.
Comfortable Seating
  • Seat is a flat plate.
  • Seat hinges on a pair of supporting parallel posts.
Elegant and Compact Aesthetics
  • Electric Scooter has a compact configuration.
  • Electric Scooter features simple structures.
  • Electric Scooter features foldable design.
  • Handlebar Assembly collapses for a streamlined look.
  • Rear Tire revolves inwardly to maintain a sleek profile.
Enhanced Portability and Storage
  • Electric Scooter features Foldable design.
  • Handlebar Assembly collapses for reduced size.
  • Rear Tire revolves inwardly to minimize folded dimensions.
  • Dolly Wheels allow easy maneuvering when folded.
  • Hitch Assembly is compact and easily transportable.
Forward and Backward Motor Control
  • Circuit Board is electrically connected to the potential regulator and the electric motor.
  • Electric Motor enables forward and backward movement.
Intuitive Control and Haptic Feedback
  • Control Lever is mounted with a torsion spring.
  • Torsion Spring provides a recoil force to the control lever.
  • Haptic Feedback ensures return to neutral position.
Robust Braking System
  • Brake Pads provide mechanical braking contact with wheels.
  • Computational Module is electrically connected to each brake pad.
  • Computational Module is adapted to receive electrical signals.
Secure Temporary Locking Mechanism
  • System allows unlocking only by the 'target account' that initially unlocked the scooter.
  • 'Target Account' unlocks scooter after 'first target operation' within 'target duration'.
  • Security Feature prevents unauthorized control during temporary stops.
Stable and Adaptable Chassis Design
  • Chassis features a carrying surface.
  • Carrying Surface supports the lower surface of the scooter body.
  • Carrying Surface prevents tilting or wobbling.
  • Drive Train Assembly is drive train assembly 110.
  • Base provides a stable platform for mounting components.
  • Potential Regulator is attached to the base.
Versatile Towing Capability
  • Electric Scooter can transport towable items such as trailers, luggage, carts.
  • Hitch Assembly allows attachment of towable items.
  • Hitch Assembly is configured for quick attachment and removal.
