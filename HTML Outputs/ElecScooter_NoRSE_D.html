Requirement Features & Specifications
Ensure durability and weather resistance
  • Frame is Powder-coated for corrosion resistance.
  • Electronics are Water-resistant (IPX4 rating).
  • Battery is Protected from overcharging and overheating.
  • Cables are Internally routed for protection.
Ensure portability and ease of storage
  • Folding Mechanism is Quick and easy to use.
  • Weight is Under 30 lbs.
  • Folded Dimensions are Compact for storage in small spaces.
  • Handlebars are Foldable.
  • Kickstand is Durable and stable.
Meet safety standards and regulations
  • Product complies_with EN17128 safety standard.
  • Battery complies_with UN38.3 battery safety standard.
  • Brakes meet Minimum braking distance requirements.
  • Lighting meets Visibility requirements for night riding.
Offer a comfortable and ergonomic design
  • Handlebars are Adjustable height.
  • Grips are_made_of Ergonomic rubber.
  • Deck is Wide enough for comfortable foot placement.
  • Display is Clear and easy to read.
  • Throttle is Ergonomic thumb throttle.
Offer smart features and connectivity
  • Display shows Speed, battery level, distance traveled.
  • Connectivity includes Bluetooth connectivity to mobile app.
  • Mobile App allows Remote locking/unlocking, ride tracking, customization of settings.
  • Security includes Anti-theft alarm system.
  • Motor Controller supports Regenerative braking.
Provide a safe and stable riding experience
  • Frame is_made_of Aluminum alloy 6061-T6.
  • Tires are 10-inch pneumatic tires.
  • Braking System includes Dual braking system (front electric, rear disc).
  • Deck has Anti-slip surface.
  • Lighting includes Front headlight, rear taillight, and side reflectors.
  • Suspension includes Front fork suspension.
  • Motor Controller features Smooth acceleration and deceleration control.
Provide sufficient range and speed for commuting
  • Battery is 36V 10Ah Lithium-ion.
  • Motor is 350W Brushless DC motor.
  • Maximum Speed is 18 mph (adjustable).
  • Range is Up to 20 miles (depending on conditions).
  • Charging Time is 4-5 hours.
