Design Concept F

Key Highlights

  • Frame is made of lightweight, high-strength aluminum alloy.
  • Wheelchair has automatic braking system on inclines.
  • Control System enables automatic following of the user's position.
  • Wheelchair incorporates voice control for hands-free operation.
Requirement Features & Specifications
Efficient Manufacturing and Assembly
  • Compact Arrangement reduces number and length of wires.
  • Integrated Design simplifies manufacture and assembly work around the wheel hub.
  • Wheelchair utilizes snap-fit components for quick assembly.
  • Wheelchair is designed for automated manufacturing processes.
Enhanced User Comfort and Safety
  • Armrests are located on each side of the seat.
  • Wheelchair includes mechanism to absorb shocks and vibrations from uneven terrain.
  • Armrest Design supports sling and patient's weight during transfer.
  • Wheelchair is equipped with obstacle avoidance sensor.
  • Wheelchair features integrated seatbelt and safety harness.
  • Wheelchair has automatic braking system on inclines.
  • Wheelchair includes emergency stop button.
Enhanced User Independence and Mobility
  • Wheelchair possesses autonomous following capability.
  • Control System uses sensor data and mobile identification device.
  • Control System enables automatic following of the user's position.
  • Electric Driving Unit is located at front portion of bottom support posts.
  • Electric Driving Unit includes outer box, battery, two motors, and control box.
  • Wheelchair is compatible with various surfaces like beds, toilets, and hospital beds.
  • Wheelchair features adjustable height and reach.
  • Wheelchair incorporates voice control for hands-free operation.
  • Wheelchair has integrated GPS and navigation system for outdoor use.
Optimized Space Utilization and Ergonomics
  • Supplemental Drive Force System has compact and rational arrangement.
  • Components are arranged around axle of the hub and stationary plate of each drive wheel.
  • Wheelchair features foldable seat in the middle of the body bracket.
  • Standing Platform is connected to the bottom of the body bracket.
  • Armrests are ergonomically designed and adjustable.
  • Footrests are foldable and adjustable.
  • Wheelchair has adjustable backrest for optimal lumbar support.
Robust Structural Integrity and Stability
  • Frame is configured to support a seat.
  • Controller is located on at least one armrest for controlling the entire electric wheelchair.
  • Elbow and Bottom Support Posts are arranged in parallel.
  • Bottom Plate connects car body support posts.
  • Wheelchair has four-wheel configuration (two front electric drive wheels, two rear wheels).
  • Frame is made of lightweight, high-strength aluminum alloy.
  • Wheelchair has low center of gravity for enhanced stability.
Simplified Maintenance and Repair
  • Controller allows for easy removal or replacement.
  • Clutch switches power transmission path between electric motor and drivable wheel.
  • Clutch has engagement and disengagement states.
  • Battery provides power supply.
  • Comparing Circuit combines armature voltage and reference signal.
  • Comparing Circuit produces a first difference signal.
  • Wheelchair utilizes modular component design for easy part replacement.
  • Diagnostic System provides real-time feedback on system health via mobile app.
