Design Concept A

Key Highlights

  • Smart sensors detect PM2.5, VOCs, and allergens.
  • Air Purifier adjusts fan speed automatically.
  • Air Purifier includes automatic UV shut-off.
  • Automatic UV shut-off activates when the unit is opened.
RequirementFeatures & Specifications
Adaptive and efficient air purification based on real-time pollution levels
  • Air Purifier utilizes smart sensors.
  • Smart sensors detect PM2.5, VOCs, and allergens.
  • Air Purifier adjusts fan speed automatically.
  • Fan speed is_based_on pollution levels.
  • Air Purifier employs independent dual-channel system.
  • Dual-channel system enables simultaneous humidification and purification.
  • Humidification uses cool mist evaporative technology.
  • Air Purifier includes drop charger.
  • Drop charger creates electrically charged water droplets.
  • Electrically charged droplets attract airborne particles.
  • Air Purifier features self-diagnostic system.
  • Self-diagnostic system monitors filter performance.
  • Air Purifier optimizes performance based on user habits.
Complete safety from UV radiation exposure
  • UV light sources are contained within the air purifier.
  • Air Purifier features UV light shield.
  • UV light shield prevents UV radiation exposure.
  • Air Purifier includes automatic UV shut-off.
  • Automatic UV shut-off activates when the unit is opened.
Durable, high-performance, and long-lasting filter membrane
  • Filter membrane is_made_of HEPA quality nano material.
  • Filter membrane possesses high chemical stability.
  • Filter membrane is acid and alkali resistant.
  • Filter membrane exhibits anti-corrosion properties.
  • Filter membrane has anti-bacterial functions.
  • Filter membrane is waterproof.
  • Filter membrane is water vapor permeable.
  • Filter membrane is windproof.
  • Filter membrane is breathable.
  • Filter membrane is UV resistant.
Effortless and efficient filter cleaning mechanism
  • Air Purifier includes detachable cleaning member.
  • Cleaning member connects_to vacuum cleaner.
  • Cleaning member guides contaminants to vacuum cleaner.
  • Filter unit is rotatably disposed.
  • Filter unit rotates_between air blowing unit and air inlet.
  • Rotation exposes entire filter surface for cleaning.
  • Rotation allows_for partial cleaning during operation.
  • Air Purifier features filter cleaning reminder.
Intuitive and comprehensive user control and monitoring
  • Air Purifier features touchscreen control panel.
  • Control panel controls fan speed.
  • Control panel controls adjustable opening (air flow).
  • Air Purifier includes air quality indicator.
  • Air quality indicator displays real-time pollution levels.
  • Air Purifier includes filter life indicator.
  • Air Purifier features tracking device.
  • Tracking device tracks user's location/proximity.
  • Air Purifier optimizes air purification based on user proximity.
Seamless integration with smart home ecosystems and user devices
  • Air Purifier connects_to smart home systems.
  • Air Purifier is_compatible_with remote controllers.
  • Air Purifier is_compatible_with smart switches.
  • Air Purifier is_compatible_with smart devices (phones, tablets).
  • Air Purifier supports wireless communication (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth).
  • Air Purifier integrates_with smart home control appliances.
  • Air Purifier integrates_with sensing devices.
  • Air Purifier includes built-in module for control.
  • Air Purifier features automatic optimization based on user habits.
  • Air Purifier features automatic optimization based on physical parameters.
Simplified installation, removal, cleaning, and part replacement processes
  • Air Purifier features modular design.
  • Modular design facilitates easy installation.
  • Modular design facilitates easy removal.
  • Modular design facilitates easy cleaning.
  • Modular design facilitates easy part replacement.
  • Components are color-coded.
  • Color-coding assists with assembly and disassembly.
  • Air Purifier includes quick-release mechanisms.
