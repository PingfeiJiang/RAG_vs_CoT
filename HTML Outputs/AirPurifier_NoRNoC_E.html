Design Concept E

Key Highlights

  • HEPA filter captures PM2.5 particles.
  • Air Quality Sensor measures PM2.5 level.
  • Housing is_made_of Recycled ABS Plastic.
  • Air passes_through Activated Carbon Filter.
Requirement Features & Specifications
Aesthetically Pleasing Design
  • Housing is_made_of Recycled ABS Plastic.
  • Housing has Minimalist Design.
  • Housing has Neutral Color Palette.
  • Product has Smooth Surfaces.
  • Product has Rounded Edges.
Child Safety Features
  • Air Purifier has Child Lock Function.
  • Child Lock Function disables Touchscreen Controls.
  • Filter Compartment has Secure Latch.
  • Latch requires Tool for opening.
Easy Filter Replacement
  • Filter Compartment is Easily Accessible.
  • Filter is Modular.
  • Filter has Pull Tab.
  • User can_easily_remove Used Filter.
  • User can_easily_insert New Filter.
Effective Particulate Matter (PM2.5) Filtration
  • Air Intake draws_in Air.
  • Air passes_through Pre-filter.
  • Pre-filter captures Large particles (dust, pet dander).
  • Air passes_through HEPA filter.
  • HEPA filter captures PM2.5 particles.
  • Clean Air is_released_from Air Outlet.
Energy Efficiency
  • Fan is Energy-efficient.
  • Power Consumption is Low.
  • Air Purifier has Automatic Mode.
  • Automatic Mode adjusts Fan Speed.
  • Fan Speed is_adjusted_based_on Air Quality.
Filter Replacement Indicator
  • Microcontroller tracks Filter Usage Time.
  • Microcontroller calculates Remaining Filter Life.
  • Microcontroller displays Filter Life Percentage.
  • Microcontroller sends Filter Replacement Reminder.
  • Reminder is_sent_via Touchscreen Display.
  • Reminder is_sent_via Mobile App.
Odor and VOC (Volatile Organic Compound) Removal
  • Air passes_through Activated Carbon Filter.
  • Activated Carbon Filter adsorbs Odors.
  • Activated Carbon Filter adsorbs VOCs.
  • Filter Material is High-quality granular activated carbon.
Quiet Operation
  • Fan is Brushless DC motor.
  • Fan operates_with Low Noise Level.
  • Housing is_designed_for Noise Dampening.
  • Airflow is Optimized for minimal turbulence.
Smart Air Quality Monitoring and Control
  • Air Purifier has Air Quality Sensor.
  • Air Quality Sensor measures PM2.5 level.
  • Air Quality Sensor measures VOC level.
  • Air Quality Sensor sends_data_to Microcontroller.
  • Microcontroller controls Fan Speed.
  • Microcontroller displays Air Quality Index (AQI).
  • User can_set Target AQI.
  • Microcontroller adjusts Fan Speed.
  • Fan Speed is_adjusted_to Maintain Target AQI.
User-Friendly Interface
  • Air Purifier has Touchscreen Display.
  • Touchscreen Display shows AQI.
  • Touchscreen Display shows Filter Life.
  • Touchscreen Display allows_user_to_set Fan Speed.
  • Touchscreen Display allows_user_to_set Timer.
  • Air Purifier has Mobile App Integration.
  • Mobile App allows_remote_control_of Air Purifier.
  • Mobile App provides Air Quality History.
