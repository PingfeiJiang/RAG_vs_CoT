Design Concept A

Key Highlights

  • Control system enables Automatic following of user's position.
  • Wheelchair uses Closed-loop feedback system.
  • Feedback system measures Motor speed using armature voltage and current.
  • Control system relies on Sensor data and mobile identification device.
Requirement Features & Specifications
Autonomous Following Capability
  • Wheelchair uses Closed-loop feedback system.
  • Feedback system measures Motor speed using armature voltage and current.
  • Control system enables Automatic following of user's position.
  • Control system relies on Sensor data and mobile identification device.
  • Electric driving unit is located at Front portion of bottom support posts.
  • Electric driving unit includes Outer box, battery, two motors, and control box.
Compact Supplemental Drive Force System
  • Components are arranged around Axle of the hub.
  • Components include Man power detect, signal transmit, control, drive motor, and drive force transmit.
  • Arrangement is around Stationary plate of each drive wheel.
Easy Controller Removal/Replacement
  • Wheelchair features Clutch mechanism.
  • Clutch switches Power transmission path between electric motor and drivable wheel.
  • Clutch has Engagement and disengagement states.
  • Wheelchair uses Battery for power supply.
  • Comparing circuit subtractively combines Armature voltage and reference signal.
  • Comparing circuit produces First difference signal.
  • Control system provides Integrated control.
Improved Mass Producibility
  • Compact arrangement reduces Number and length of wires.
  • Integrated design around the wheel hub simplifies Manufacture and assembly work.
Safety Features
  • Wheelchair equipped with Obstacle avoidance sensor.
Seated Option
  • Seat has Armrests on each side.
  • Seat is Foldable in the middle of the body bracket.
  • Standing platform is connected to Bottom of the body bracket.
Shock and Vibration Absorption
  • Armrest design contributes to Shock and vibration absorption.
  • Armrests are structurally sound and positioned for Supporting sling and patient's weight during transfer.
Stable Base
  • Elbow and bottom support posts are arranged in Parallel.
  • Bottom plate connects Car body support posts.
  • Wheelchair has Four-wheel configuration.
  • Four-wheel configuration includes Two front electric drive wheels and two rear wheels.
Structural Support
  • Frame supports Seat.
  • Controller is located on At least one armrest.
  • Controller controls Entire electric wheelchair.
Surface Compatibility
  • Wheelchair is compatible with Various surfaces like beds, toilets, and hospital beds.
  • Compatibility implies Range of adjustable height and reach.
