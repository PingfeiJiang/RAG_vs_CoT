Design Concept B

Key Highlights

  • Air Purifier optimizes_automatically_based_on user habits.
  • Air Purifier optimizes_automatically_based_on physical parameters.
  • Air Purifier can_be_controlled_via wireless communication device.
  • Air Purifier performs air purification.
RequirementFeatures & Specifications
Adaptive Air Purification & Humidification
  • Air Purifier performs air purification.
  • Air Purifier performs cool mist evaporative humidification.
  • Air Purifier adapts_to pollution levels.
  • Air Purifier utilizes independent dual-channel design.
  • Dual-channel design prevents inefficient simultaneous filtration and purification.
  • Drop charger creates electrically charged drops of aqueous solution.
  • Electrically charged drops attract_and_capture airborne particles.
  • Control panel manages fan speed.
  • Control panel manages opening size.
Durable and High-Performance Filter
  • Filter membrane is acid and alkali resistant.
  • Filter membrane has anti-corrosion function.
  • Filter membrane has anti-bacteria function.
  • Filter membrane is HEPA quality nano membrane.
  • Filter membrane has high chemical stability.
  • Filter membrane is waterproof.
  • Filter membrane is water vapor permeable.
  • Filter membrane is windproof.
  • Filter membrane is breathable.
Ease of Use: Installation, Removal, Cleaning, and Replacement
  • Air Purifier facilitates simple and convenient installation.
  • Air Purifier facilitates simple and convenient removal.
  • Air Purifier facilitates simple and convenient cleaning.
  • Air Purifier facilitates simple and convenient part replacement.
Efficient Filter Cleaning
  • Cleaning member is_detachably_connected_to vacuum cleaner.
  • Cleaning member is_disposed_in_proximity_to filter unit.
  • Cleaning member guides contaminants to vacuum cleaner.
  • Filter unit is_rotatably_disposed_between air blowing unit and air inlet.
  • Filter unit rotation exposes entire filter surface for cleaning.
  • Rotating filter allows_for cleaning of a portion of the filter while other parts continue to filter air.
Smart Integration and Control
  • Air inlet is_located_on back surface.
  • Air Purifier optimizes_automatically_based_on user habits.
  • Air Purifier optimizes_automatically_based_on physical parameters.
  • Air Purifier can_be_controlled_via remote controller.
  • Air Purifier can_be_controlled_via smart switch.
  • Air Purifier can_be_controlled_via smart device.
  • Air Purifier can_be_controlled_via wireless communication device.
  • Air Purifier can_be_controlled_via smart home control appliance.
  • Air Purifier can_be_controlled_via sensing device.
  • Air Purifier can_be_controlled_via built-in module.
  • Air Purifier is_jointed_with_surfaces_on side.
UV Radiation Safety
  • UV light sources are_contained_within air purifier.
  • Containment prevents exposure to nearby persons.
User Control and Customization
  • Control panel controls fan.
  • Control panel controls adjustable opening.
  • Tracking device tracks user's location.
  • Tracking device tracks user's proximity.
