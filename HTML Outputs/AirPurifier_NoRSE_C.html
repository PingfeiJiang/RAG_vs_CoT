Requirement Features & Specifications
Aesthetically pleasing design to blend with home decor
  • Air Purifier has Modern and minimalist design.
  • Air Purifier is_available_in Multiple color options.
  • Air Purifier has Compact form factor.
  • Air Purifier has Integrated handle for portability.
Easy filter replacement and maintenance
  • Filter compartment is Easily accessible.
  • Filters are Easy to remove and replace.
  • Air Purifier has Filter replacement indicator.
  • Filter replacement indicator alerts User when filter needs replacement.
  • Air Purifier has Washable pre-filter.
  • Pre-filter can_be Washed and reused.
Effective air purification for various pollutants (dust, pollen, smoke, odors, VOCs)
  • Air Purifier has_filter Pre-filter.
  • Pre-filter removes Large particles (dust, hair).
  • Air Purifier has_filter HEPA filter.
  • HEPA filter removes Fine particles (pollen, PM2.5).
  • Air Purifier has_filter Activated carbon filter.
  • Activated carbon filter absorbs Odors and VOCs.
  • Air Purifier has_technology Optional UV-C light.
  • UV-C light kills Bacteria and viruses.
  • Air Purifier has_sensor Air quality sensor.
  • Air quality sensor measures PM2.5, VOCs.
  • Air Purifier adjusts Fan speed based on air quality sensor readings.
Energy efficiency to minimize electricity consumption
  • Fan is Energy-efficient fan.
  • Motor is Energy-efficient motor.
  • Air Purifier has Automatic mode.
  • Automatic mode adjusts Fan speed based on air quality.
  • Air Purifier has Sleep mode.
  • Air Purifier meets Energy Star certification standards.
Quiet operation for use in bedrooms and living spaces
  • Fan is Low-noise fan.
  • Motor is Brushless DC motor.
  • Air Purifier has Sound dampening materials.
  • Air Purifier has_mode Sleep mode.
  • Sleep mode sets Fan speed to lowest setting.
  • Sleep mode dims Indicator lights.
Safety features to prevent accidents and ensure safe operation
  • Air Purifier has Tip-over protection.
  • Air Purifier has Overheat protection.
  • Air Purifier meets Safety standards (e.g., UL, CE).
  • Air Purifier is_made_of Flame-retardant materials.
User-friendly interface and controls
  • Air Purifier has Touchscreen control panel.
  • Control panel displays Air quality readings.
  • Control panel allows Manual fan speed adjustment.
  • Control panel allows Timer setting.
  • Control panel allows Filter replacement indicator reset.
  • Air Purifier has Mobile app connectivity.
  • Mobile app allows Remote control.
  • Mobile app provides Air quality history.
  • Mobile app sends Filter replacement reminders.
